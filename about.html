About The Lost Forge

_The Lost Forge_ is more than a modding project — it's a myth reborn. Inspired by worlds like **Skyrim**, **HTTYD**, and the old tales of the North, this realm brings you handcrafted weapons, lorebound relics, and community-created magic.

🗺️ Origins

Forged in the minds of fans who grew up with dragons and steel, this world blends fantasy, Viking myth, and game modding into one living forge.

🧙‍♂️ The Creators

We're a small group of creators, artists and writers who love storytelling through code. Each mod is a story, each update a new legend.

🔥 Join the Forge

Whether you’re a player or a creator, you’re welcome at the Forge. Share your ideas, shape the future of this world, and leave your mark in fire and rune.
